Project Overview

Abstract

The proposed system monitors voltage, current, and power in a distribution feeder and performs automatic load control based on threshold values. This approach enhances reliability, reduces power loss, and minimizes manual intervention.

Problem Statement

Traditional manual monitoring of electrical distribution feeders leads to delayed detection of overloads and faults, causing equipment damage, power interruptions, and reduced system reliability.

Solution

Continuous real-time monitoring of electrical parameters with automated threshold-based load control to prevent overloads and ensure system reliability.
